Wilderness Ramblers

Nature and Service for 8-9 Year Olds

Jersey City co-ed scouting for kids ages 6-9!

The Wilderness Ramblers are for you if you and your child want to:

  • Go camping
  • Go on hikes and outings in nature
  • Learn to respect the environment and other children
  • Earn badges in safety, nature, crafting, and service

         

We are part of the Outdoor Service Guides. OSG is coed, welcoming, and parent-led.


What we do!

  • Focus on fun in nature, learning skills, and being a good civic and environmental citizen
  • Go on monthly “rambles”: hikes outside of Jersey City in the great outdoors or educational trips
  • Meet one evening a month for a game and to work on skills and earn badges
  • Bring the whole family camping twice a year

This group is for you

We are for families new to the outdoors and who are already passionate. You do not need camping gear to get started. to get started

Who Can Participate

We have a Timberwolf section for kids 8 to 9 years old. Family Camp is an all-family activity. Parents may bring older or younger siblings to events if that is helpful.

When We Meet

  • 2nd Sunday of Month 11am: ramble (often 30-45 minutes outside of Jersey City)
  • 3rd Wednesday of the Month 6pm: near Van Vorst Park
  • 2 Family Camps: September and May
  • 2 Field Days: October and June

Dues

$425/year per family for uniform, materials, and shared gear. Additionally, each child and adult participant pays a $80/year contribution toward the group's insurance. Inquire about no-questions-asked scholarships.
