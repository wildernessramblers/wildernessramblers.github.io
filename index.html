Wilderness Ramblers

Nature and Service for 6-8 Year Olds

We are a Jersey City-based co-ed scouting group for kids ages 6-8!

The Wilderness Ramblers are for you if you and your child want to:
    - Go camping
    - Go on hikes and outings in nature
    - Learn to respect the environment and other children
    - Earn badges in safety, nature, crafting, and service

         

We are part of the Outdoor Service Guides. OSG is coed, welcoming, and parent led


What we do!

  • Focus on fun in nature, learning skills, and being a good civic and environmental citizen
  • Go on monthly “rambles”: hikes outside of Jersey City in the great outdoors or educational trips
  • Meet one evening a month for a game and to work on skills and earn badges
  • Bring the whole family camping twice a year

This group is for you!

We are for families new to the outdoors and who are already passionate. You do not need camping gear to get started. OSGWildernessRamblers@gmail.com to get started
